# AI Product Demo Planning

Design small demos that prove product judgment, not just prompt-writing.

## Workflow

1. Read the target JD and choose the strongest product direction: Agent, AI search, AIGC, workflow automation, data copilot, or companion.
2. Define the user and one painful task.
3. Scope the demo to one complete loop:
   - input.
   - reasoning or retrieval.
   - tool/action.
   - output.
   - evaluation.
4. Add product artifacts:
   - PRD.
   - user flow.
   - prompt or tool design.
   - evaluation set.
   - demo script.
   - metrics plan.
5. Keep engineering scope small enough to finish in days, not weeks.
6. Prepare a resume bullet and interview explanation.

Read `references/demo-scorecard.md` to choose what to build.
